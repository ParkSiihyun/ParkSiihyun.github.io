📖 Glossary Page

A sample of the /glossary page — plain-language finance terms.

CAPM Capital Asset Pricing Model

A model for the required return on equity: Re = Rf + β(Rm − Rf). The most common way to estimate cost of equity in a DCF.

DCF Discounted Cash Flow

Intrinsic valuation method. Forecast unlevered free cash flows, discount by WACC, add a terminal value. The gold standard — and also the method most sensitive to inputs.

EBITDA

Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation & Amortization. A proxy for operating cash flow that's independent of capital structure.

Enterprise Value EV

Market cap + debt + preferred + minority interest − cash. Total value to all capital providers.

🧮 Formula Cheatsheet

A sample of the /formulas page — core valuation math.

WACC

WACC = (E/V) × Rₑ + (D/V) × Rd × (1 − T)

Blended after-tax cost of capital. Weights use market values, not book.

Unlevered Free Cash Flow

FCFF = EBIT × (1 − T) + D&A − CapEx − Δ Working Capital

Cash flow to all investors before financing. Numerator in an unlevered DCF.

Terminal Value (Gordon Growth)

TV = FCF(n+1) / (WACC − g)

Keep g at or below long-run GDP growth (~2–3%).
